Proof-of-Reserves

Merkle-tree attestations anchored on-chain. Third-party verification published on a rolling basis (demo).

How does PoR work?

Customer liabilities are anonymized and hashed as leaves in a Merkle tree. Verifiers recompute roots to ensure all balances are included without exposing identities.

What about liabilities?

We publish methods to independently validate both assets and liabilities to avoid partial attestations.

When are reports updated?

Weekly, with historical snapshots retained for comparison (demo).
